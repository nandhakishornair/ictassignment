Top places to visit in Thrissur

Athirapally falls

Exuding a mystique and serene vibe, Athirappilly Waterfalls are the most famous falls in the state of Kerala. Located about 63 km from Thrissur and 70 km from Kochi, they are a popular picnic spot and lie near the Vazhachal Waterfalls, which are another delightful sight.

Snehatheeram

he stunning Thalikulam Snehatheeram Beach is located about 25 km or 1 hour ride from Thrissur and is loved for its natural ambience and wonderful visuals of the coast. It is one of the best maintained beaches in Kerala.

for more info click here
Vadakkunnathan temple

The temple is famous for the rarity of the temple murals, of which the Vasukishayana and Nrithanatha murals are of great importance and are worshipped daily. The temple also houses a museum of ancient wall paintings, wood carvings and art pieces of ancient times

for more info click here
Kerala kalamandalam

Kerala Kalamandalam has been regarded as the most prestigious Kathakali school in the entire world. This legendary school of art is located in Cheruthuruthy and was originally set up by the famous poet of Malayalam, Vallathol Narayana Menon.

Shobha city

A small mall but befits the place of thrissur for it sharing its calm culture and great food. There is good eatery and decent shopping destination. There is ample parking, corniche , rides for kids, shopping, cafes and patios to have fun and chat.

Chavakkad beach

Not far away from Guruvayur, is this beach. The place is mostly a fishing port and the walkway to the beach is by crossing the fishery transport units on either sides. Once you enter the beach, the colourful boats are a beautiful sight and best place for photography
